How does Elon Musk Tweets?

Musk has used Twitter as a direct communication channel with the public and investors, often making significant announcements related to his various companies, including Tesla and SpaceX. This has had a polarizing effect, with some praising his transparency and accessibility, while others criticize his impulsiveness and the potential market volatility his tweets can trigger. One of the most notable aspects of Musk's Twitter activity is his sense of humor and willingness to engage with his followers, responding to questions and memes in a personable manner. This has endeared him to many and created a unique brand of internet culture around his online presence. However, Musk's Twitter management has also led to several high-profile controversies, including legal battles and regulatory scrutiny over tweets that may have influenced stock prices. This has raised questions about the responsibilities and consequences associated with the unchecked power of influential figures on social media.
